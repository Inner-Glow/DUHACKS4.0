About
Our project is an innovative platform designed specifically for college events. It allows event organizers to post details about their events, manage registrations, and sell tickets seamlessly. Students can easily browse upcoming events, book tickets, and stay updated on campus activities. With a user-friendly interface and secure transactions, our platform enhances event participation and fosters a more connected college community.
